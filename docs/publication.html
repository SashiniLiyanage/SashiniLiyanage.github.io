Datasets

This section lists the datasets acquired and processed in relation to the research publications made publicly available by the authors.


  • A Multi-Sensory Inertial Measurement Unit Dataset for Fetal Condition Monitoring

    This dataset comprises abdominal movement recordings from 44 pregnant mothers, captured using a multi-sensory device with four IMUs (MPU6050 sensors) that measure tri-axial acceleration and gyroscopic data. Ground truth fetal movement labels were obtained via ultrasound observation and maternal perception, both under expert supervision, and all data is provided in .csv format.

    doi: 10.7910/DVN/QHFHYC

  • Dataset of Annotated Oral Cavity Images for Oral Cancer Detection

    This dataset includes 3,000 oral cavity images with detailed patient metadata, categorized into oral cancer, OPMD, benign, and healthy groups. Images feature annotated lesion boundaries and are available in COCO format

    doi: 10.5281/zenodo.10664055

  • Spatio-Temporal Activity Data Across Professional Classes in Kandy, Sri Lanka

    This dataset contains GPS-based motion data from 100 individuals in Kandy District, Sri Lanka, spanning roughly 21 days across 13 professional categories. Each individual's visited locations are clustered into 18 unique sites within seven zones (e.g., Industrial, Commercial, Educational), with minute-by-minute timestamps documenting their movements.

    doi: 10.5281/zenodo.13621863
